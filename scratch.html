Weekly rhythm

I stay consistent when the week has a shape. Below, an ordered list uses nested unordered lists for concrete actions (one valid nested-list pattern).

  1. Protect sleep
    • Same wake time most days (even after late shifts).
    • Phone across the room so “one more scroll” doesn’t win.
  2. Move early
    • 10-minute walk or mobility before the day gets loud.
    • Water bottle filled the night before—small win, real momentum.
  3. Strength twice a week
    • Compound lifts or bodyweight basics I can track.
    • Stop before sloppy reps; quality beats ego.

Alternate nested lists

Here is the other valid pattern: an unordered list with a nested ordered list inside.

  • Low-energy day
    1. Five-minute walk outside.
    2. One glass of water before coffee.
    3. One song of stretching or mobility.
  • High-stress day
    1. Short breathing reset (slow exhale).
    2. One small win: make the bed or clear the desk.
    3. Move the body before scrolling the phone.
